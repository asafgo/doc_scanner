Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 12, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how our Application handles user information, device permissions, and document data.

1. Complete Local Processing (No Cloud Storage)

The Application is designed to operate strictly as a local productivity tool.

  • No Server Collection: We do not own, operate, or use external servers to collect, store, track, or monitor your personal data.
  • No Personally Identifiable Information (PII): We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, accounts, or physical locations.

2. Device Permissions and Data Handling

To provide document scanning and PDF generation features, the Application requires certain device capabilities. All processing happens entirely on your local hardware:

  • Camera Access: The Application requires access to your device's camera to enable the document scanning feature (powered by Google ML Kit). The camera feed is used strictly in real-time to detect document edges and capture the page layout. No images or video feeds are ever transmitted to external servers or third parties.
  • Storage and Files: Documents, raw images, and generated PDFs are processed and stored locally within the application's isolated sandbox directory or temporary cache. We have no access to your files or generated documents.
  • Local Preferences: We use local on-device key-value storage to save your app preferences, UI states, or configuration choices. This data is never transmitted to external servers or third parties..
